I'm signed up, what do I do?

    When you know your pairing

  • Find and message the player that you've been paired with (in game, or discord if you know them there)
  • Decide a time that works for both of you to meet in Ulthuan
  • Choose who is the attacker, and who is the defender

    • When you meet in Ulthuan

  • The defender will need to send exactly 500 nurglings to a tile (no other troops, no warlords, no champions)
  • The attacker then needs to tile hit with exactly 500 nurglings (no other troops, no warlords, no champions)

  • Get back to Fuit Gummy with the report so that the winner can be put into their next pairing
