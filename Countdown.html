What are The Nurgling Games?

During the Chaos and Conquest Ulthuan event, The Nurgling Games will take place, a tournament that will have players battle with their nurglings to find the one true nurgling king.


What do I need to take part

  • Be able to invade Ulthuan (k20, invade pass or whatever)
  • Be willing to unshield for your fights
  • 500 nurglings

How do I sign up

-If you're in the Chaos and Conquest discord, message Fuit Gummy with your in game name, region and alliance
